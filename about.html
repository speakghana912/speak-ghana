About Speak Ghana

Our story, our values, and the people dedicated to helping you reconnect with your roots

Our Story

Speak Ghana was born from a simple observation: millions of people in the diaspora long to connect with their Ghanaian heritage, but don't know where to start. Language is the key that unlocks identity, culture, and belonging.

Founded by Richard Asiamah in 2024, our school began with one simple goal: to help diaspora learners speak Twi confidently and reconnect with their roots. What started as a few private lessons has grown into a community of hundreds of learners worldwide.

Today, we offer structured courses in Twi, Ewe, Fante, Ga, and Dagbani, with plans to expand to Yoruba, Hausa, Swahili, and more. But our mission remains the same: one word at a time, we're bringing people home.

"We believe that language is the key that unlocks identity, heritage, and belonging. Every word learned is a step closer to home."

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Who are your classes for?

Anyone who wants to learn Twi! We specialize in diaspora learners, but welcome everyone.

Do I need any materials?

Just Zoom and WhatsApp. All PDFs and textbook are provided free.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

PayPal, Wise, Mobile Money, and Bank Transfer.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect next month.

Classes & Scheduling

What if I miss a class?

Recordings for group classes. Private lessons reschedule with 24h notice.

How do group schedules work?

We find a time that works for the majority. You vote on the final schedule.

Teachers & Support

What if I don't like my teacher?

Request a change - we'll find you a better match at no extra cost.

Are teachers native speakers?

Yes! All our teachers are native Twi speakers from Ghana.
