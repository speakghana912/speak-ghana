Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Speak One, Learn One Scholarship?

Ghana Card holders who already speak one Ghanaian language get 25% off to learn another. Includes diaspora returnees with Ghana Cards.

What is the Certificate of Achievement?

Complete 6 months of continuous learning and receive an official certificate signed by our founders, showing your language level and dedication.

Do you have classes for kids?

Yes! We have specialized programs for ages 5-12 (Twi Tots, Twi Explorers) and teens 13-17.

What if I miss a class?

Recordings are available for group classes. Private lessons can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice.

Can siblings learn together?

Absolutely! Our Siblings Special package is designed for families with multiple children.

What payment methods do you accept?

Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), Bank Transfer for Ghana. PayPal, Wise, and international transfers for diaspora.
