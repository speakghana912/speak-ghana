About Sarah

Sarah Seyram Arhinful is a dedicated and versatile educator with over 9 years of teaching experience in the Ghanaian basic education system. She has a proven track record of fostering academic excellence and implementing modern pedagogical strategies. Passionate about inculcating acceptable values and impacting lives, Sarah brings warmth, professionalism, and expertise to every class.

Her students consistently praise her patience, deep knowledge of Ewe language and culture, and ability to make learning enjoyable and accessible for learners of all ages — especially diaspora learners reconnecting with their roots.

💬 Why Learn Ewe with Sarah?

"Ewe is a beautiful language rich with proverbs, rhythm, and cultural depth. I don't just teach words — I teach the stories, the songs, and the traditions that make Ewe come alive. My goal is to help you speak with confidence and connect with Ewe-speaking communities across Ghana, Togo, and Benin. Every student who walks through my virtual door leaves with more than just vocabulary — they leave with a piece of home." — Sarah Seyram Arhinful
