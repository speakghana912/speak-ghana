Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about corporate training

How many learners can you accommodate?

We can train groups from 5 to 50+ learners. Larger groups receive additional discounts.

Can you customize the curriculum?

Absolutely. We can focus on Business Twi, Medical Twi, or any specific context your team needs.

Do you offer in-person training?

Yes, in Accra and Kumasi. We can also deliver training at your office or preferred location.

What materials are included?

PDFs, worksheets, audio files, and digital certificates. Premium package includes branded certificates.

How do you measure progress?

We provide monthly progress reports, including attendance, participation, and language assessments.

Can we do a trial session?

Yes. We offer a complimentary 60-minute demo session for organizations considering our training.
