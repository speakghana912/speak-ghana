🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Speaking with flat English-like tones (no pitch change)
  • ❌ Ignoring vowel length - "pa" (good) vs "paa" (very much)
  • ❌ Confusing "ko" (fight) with "kɔ" (go)
  • ❌ Mixing up "gy", "dw", and "tw" sounds
  • ❌ Using the same tone for all syllables in a word
  • ❌ Confusing "bu" (break) with "bɔ" (hit/create)

💡 Practice these minimal pairs daily. Listen carefully to the tone differences!
