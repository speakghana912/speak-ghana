Our Mission & Vision

“Mission

To reconnect the global African diaspora with their roots through language, culture, and community – one word at a time.

We believe that language is the key that unlocks identity, heritage, and belonging. Every word learned is a step closer to home.

“Vision

To become Africa's leading online language school, offering every major African language to learners worldwide.

By 2030, We Envision:

  • 20+ African languages taught
  • 10,000+ active students globally
  • Physical learning centers in 5 African countries
  • Partnerships with diaspora organizations worldwide
  • A complete digital ecosystem (app, web, community)

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a class?

Recordings are available for group classes. Private lessons can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect the following month.

Do I need any materials?

Just a phone/computer with Zoom and WhatsApp. All PDFs and worksheets are provided, and you'll receive the complete textbook with your paid plan.

Is the textbook really free with lessons?

Absolutely! Every paid lesson plan includes the complete Speak Ghana Twi textbook (digital copy) – a 300+ page resource you can keep forever.

How do group schedules work?

Once your group is formed, we'll find a time that works for the majority. You'll vote on the final day/time.

What if I'm not happy with my teacher?

No problem! Just request a change and we'll match you with a different teacher at no extra cost.
