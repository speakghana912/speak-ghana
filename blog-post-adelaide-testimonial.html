From Syllables to Sentences: Adelaide's First Month with Speak Ghana

Adelaide Daniels (Kakra)

Speak Ghana Student, March 2026 Cohort | UK-based diaspora learner

👯‍♀️ Twin sister of Adelina (Panyin) — both learning Twi together!

I have been taking lessons for a month now, and I am now able to form simple sentences from the words I have been taught. The teachers are very attentive and help whenever I need it.

— Adelaide Daniels (Kakra)

I love that there are 2 teachers, one helps more with the speaking, and when I need something written down or need to ask simple questions without changing the flow of the lesson, another teacher is always available in the chat to answer.

I feel confident reading the alphabet and I love how vocabulary is so naturally built throughout the lessons. The teachers are also funny, very kind, and sweet, it makes the lessons so enjoyable and fun!

I love the pacing of the class. It's slow enough that you can go over things to ensure understanding, but fast enough that you can see the progress every week with reading, speaking, and writing!
