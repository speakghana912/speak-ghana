Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does it work?

Choose your plan, make payment, and we'll email you the password to access all course materials and games. You can start immediately.

Are the games included?

Yes! All plans include access to fun interactive games to help reinforce your learning.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled anytime. Your access continues until the end of your paid period.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Standard and Premium plans include certificates for each level. Complete all 5 levels for the "Speak Ghana Certified" credential.

What's the difference between Self-Paced and Live Classes?

Self-Paced is pre-recorded videos you watch anytime. Live classes are real-time with a teacher. Both include PDFs, worksheets, quizzes, and games.

How do I pay?

Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) to 0552263323 (Richard Asiamah), or PayPal/Wise for international payments.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium at any time. Just message us.

Where do I access my courses after enrollment?

Go to portal.speakghana.org and enter your unique password.
