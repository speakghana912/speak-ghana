Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does it work?

Choose your plan, make payment, and we'll email you the password to access all course materials, audio library, and games. You can start immediately.

Are the games included?

Games are included in Standard and Premium plans. Basic plan includes videos, PDFs, and audio library only.

What is the audio library?

The audio library has pronunciation for all Twi letters, digraphs, numbers 1-20, greetings, and introductions. Play directly in your browser — no downloads needed.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Standard and Premium plans include certificates for each level. Complete all 5 levels for the "Speak Ghana Certified" credential.

What's the difference between Basic, Standard, and Premium?

Basic: Videos + PDFs + audio library. Standard: + worksheets + quizzes + certificates + games. Premium: + monthly live Q&A with teacher.

How do I pay?

Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) to 0552263323 (Richard Asiamah), or PayPal/Wise for international payments.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium at any time. Just message us.

Where do I access my courses after enrollment?

Go to portal.speakghana.org and enter your unique password.
