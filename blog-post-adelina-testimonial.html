From London to Ghana: Adelina's Twi Journey

March 30, 2026 By Adelina Daniels (Panyin) 4 min read
👯‍♀️ Fun fact: Adelina and Adelaide are twins! Adelina is the older twin (Panyin) and Adelaide is the younger twin (Kakra). Both are learning Twi with Speak Ghana!

As a Ghanaian born in the UK, I've always felt a deep pull toward my roots. But there was one thing missing: the ability to speak Twi.

I could understand bits and pieces from what my parents said at home, but I could never respond. Every family gathering felt like I was on the outside looking in. I knew it was time to finally learn.

That's when I found Speak Ghana.

📚 One Month Later...

I've learned so much in a month and I'm glad I chose to learn with Speak Ghana. The teachers are so amazing!

— Adelina Daniels (Panyin) 🇬🇭🇬🇧

Just one month ago, I couldn't form a single sentence in Twi. Now? I'm putting together phrases, understanding more of what my family says, and feeling more connected to my heritage than ever before.

👩🏾‍🏫 Learning with Tetebea & Richard

I've been learning with Tetebea and Richard, and they are so great at matching the classes to our level and pace. They don't just teach, they truly care about our progress.

What I love most is that they are always available. During lesson time, they answer every question. And outside of lesson time? They're still there to help. It's been an absolute pleasure to be their student.

🎯 Why This Works

The classes are structured perfectly, not too fast, not too slow. Every week, I can see my progress. Reading, writing, speaking, it's all improving.

I know with their help, I'll be fluent by the time I travel back home to Ghana. And honestly? I'm so excited by that!
