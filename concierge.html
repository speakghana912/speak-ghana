Pre-arrival & on-ground support

What we offer 🇬🇭

You focus on reconnecting. We’ll handle the logistics, errands, and local details.

Airport Meet & Greet

Warm welcome at Kotoka International. We’ll handle pickup, assist with luggage, get your local SIM, and ensure a smooth transfer to your accommodation.

Property Verification

View properties on your behalf, take video tours, verify landlords, negotiate prices, and confirm legitimacy before you sign any agreement.

Shopping & Price Checks

Confirm real market prices for furniture, appliances, or gifts. We’ll shop for you or accompany you to avoid overcharging.

Documentation Support

Guidance on Ghana Card registration, visa assistance, notarizations, and liaising with local authorities.

Family Connection

Help locate relatives, arrange introductory visits, translate communications, and bridge cultural gaps.

Business & Supplier Checks

Verify suppliers, confirm pricing, vet contractors, and help you establish local partnerships with confidence.
