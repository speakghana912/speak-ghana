Every child deserves to know where they come from

Most Twi resources are designed for adults – kids get bored, teens tune out. Diaspora youth often feel disconnected from their roots, and parents struggle to pass language to the next generation.

That's why we created age-appropriate, engaging programs specifically for young learners.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Speak One, Learn One Scholarship?

Ghana Card holders who already speak one Ghanaian language get 25% off to learn another. Includes diaspora returnees with Ghana Cards.

What is the Certificate of Achievement?

Complete 6 months of continuous learning and receive an official certificate signed by our founders, showing your child's language level and dedication.

What if my child misses a class?

Recordings are available for group classes. Private lessons can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice.

Can siblings learn together?

Absolutely! Our Siblings Special package is designed for families with multiple children. Plus, sibling discount applies!

What age is best to start?

We welcome learners from age 5! Younger children learn through play, while teens focus on conversation and cultural connection.

Do you offer a trial?

Yes! Contact us to schedule a free trial class and see if our program is right for your child.

Our Commitment to Young Learners

"Sankofa – Go back and get it. For our children, for our future."

At Speak Ghana, we believe every child and teen deserves to know where they come from, speak with their grandparents, feel proud of their heritage, and belong to a community.

We don't just teach words. We build identity, confidence, and connection – one young learner at a time.
