Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the free March classes?

Just email us or WhatsApp! No registration form needed for March - we'll send you the class link directly.

Officially April 1, 2026 at 6:00 PM GMT. But you can start your paid plan anytime in March!

Recordings provided for group classes. Private lessons reschedule with 24h notice.

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect the following month.

Just Zoom and WhatsApp. All PDFs and the complete textbook are provided free.

Absolutely! Every paid plan includes our 300+ page textbook (digital copy).
